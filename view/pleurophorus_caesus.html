Comments Described by Fowler (1890) as 'somewhat doubtful as British' and stated by Stephens to be ' a rare species at least towards the eastern parts of Britain; in the western it appears to be more abundant'. 'Localities given are near Bristol, and Pentire Point, Cornwall. Hyman (1992) gives Glamorgan as a locality, and also recorded historically (1890) from the Scilly Isles. Recently imported on celery; Huntingdon, Cambridgeshire in 1997. The species has been reported as a pest of crops in the USA where it was introduced in 1871. If it was ever indigenous to Britain, which is doubtful, it is certainly extinct now.
