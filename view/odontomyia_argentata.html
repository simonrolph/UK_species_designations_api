Comments Recorded from 23 hectads since 1990. The distribution is lowland England from Somerset to Norfolk but not in the extreme south-east of England. Within this band it is found frequently, suggesting that it is probably climatically limited (although widespread from central Europe to Scandinavia and Russia). It shows no decline, no obvious contraction in range and a relatively low proportion of old and recent hectads in common, suggesting that there are probably many extant sites unvisited recently. Adults have an early flight period so may be underrecorded.
