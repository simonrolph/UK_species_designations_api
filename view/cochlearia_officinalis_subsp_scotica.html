At least 20% of international population of species or well-recognised subspecies occurring in Northern Ireland. 6. Irish Red Data Book (RDB) species classed as critically endangered (CR), endangered (EN) or vulnerable (VU). 7. Red-listed species in either Birds of Conservation Concern in Ireland (BOCCI) or the UK Birds of Conservation Concern (UK BOCC) lists.
