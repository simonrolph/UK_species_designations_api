Criteria description B2b(i,ii)
Comments The species is known from 13 post-1980 hectads in South Hampshire (the New Forest), Dorset, South Devon, East Cornwall and West Norfolk. It meets the area requirements under criterion B2 for Endangered (AOO <500 km2) and Vulnerable (AOO <2,000 km2) and has undergone a substantial decline in AOO and EOO though this may now have stabilised (John Walters, pers. comm.). It is near to the 'Number of locations' threshold for Vulnerable (B2a). The species is not known to be severely fragmented, nor to undergo extreme fluctuations but both are likely for this species of bare ground patches on heathland.
