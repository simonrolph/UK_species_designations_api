Comments Historically known from Somerset (Bath), Glamorgan (Swansea) and Essex (Chingford and Walthamstow). Hyman (1992) also gives West Kent. The dataset gives probable erroneous records from Leicestershire (Uppingham) and from Glamorgan (Merthyr-mawr Warren and Mynydd Maendy) as well as a more plausible record, from Monks Wood NNR (Hunts.) in 1873. The species probably became extinct in the early 20th Century as the last record is from Milbourne St Andrew, Dorset in 1926. Most existing Museum specimens that are of British origin are without locality and date.
